Hobbies

  • Acting I've loved and participated in acting and theater since middle school. Not only is performing tons of fun, but it is also extremely rewarding. Really stepping into a character's shoes and making them come to life is incredible.

  • Writing Ever since learning to read and write at age 3, writing has been a very relaxing hobby for me. It allows me to express my thoughts and create entire worlds with a paper and pen. I also think my neat handwriting may motivate me a bit; seeing a page filled with words makes me unreasonably happy.

  • Music I have a lot of hobbies that relate to music: playing instruments, listening to music, collecting CDs and vinyls... I seriously cannot live without it, as it's simultaneously so expressive and complex.

  • Gaming I'm not too sure if this counts as a hobby; it's more of an interest, sure... But they're really good for passing the time and challenging myself. My favorite series is the Resident Evil series, as it perfectly blends horror, puzzles, and action.

  • Drawing I love drawing, especially making original characters! I love putting my own spin on my favorite characters too, and adore making posters, stickers, and flyers!
